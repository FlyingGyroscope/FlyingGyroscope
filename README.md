## About me

I love the 3D printing hobby and my Voron 2.4R2 has around 6k hours.  I taught myself to use Autodesk (Fusion) 360 and I focus on practical designs.  However, I also like trying new, interesting designs or making things for family and friends.  I share many of my designs on [printables.com](https://www.printables.com/@FlyingGyroscope).

I learned some C++ style programming in school, and I occasionally play around with Arduino projects.

In my other free time, I enjoy Ultimate Frisbee.
