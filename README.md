## About me

I love the 3D printing hobby and my Voron 2.4R2 has around 6k hours of runtime.  I taught myself Autodesk (Fusion) 360 and I mostly focus on practical designs.  However, I also like trying new, interesting designs as well as making things for family and friends.  I share many of my designs on [printables.com](https://www.printables.com/@FlyingGyroscope).

I learned some C++ style programming in school, and I occasionally play around with Arduino projects.

In my other free time, I play Ultimate Frisbee.
